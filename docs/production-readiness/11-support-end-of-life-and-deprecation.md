# Support, End of Life, and Deprecation

Status: support framework; no support term committed  
Owner: support, release, product, security, and program

Every channel and platform records support start, security patch obligation, compatibility policy, migration guidance, deprecation notice, end-of-life date, and user communication.

A feature cannot be labeled stable without an owner and maintenance plan. Unsupported builds remain clearly labeled and cannot receive silent web-visible behavior changes.

Deprecation includes telemetry or evidence where privacy permits, alternatives, staged warnings, enterprise impact, rollback, and public rationale.
