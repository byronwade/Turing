# Production Readiness Review

Status: mandatory final review structure  
Owner: human release authority and cross-functional owners

The review verifies stable scope, requirements, risks, ADRs, owners/backups, SLOs, compatibility, security, privacy, accessibility, data integrity, update trust, provenance, legal approval, service operations, support, incident response, rollback, and known issues.

Evidence is linked by artifact digest. Exceptions are explicit, narrow, time-bounded, owned, and cannot waive critical sandbox, update, credential, data-loss, trusted-UI, accessibility, or unauthorized-action blockers.

The implementation agent may assemble the review package. It cannot approve it or promote the release.
